About

Barens Group is a private investment and holding group with operating
segments in Commodities, Real Estate and Venture Capital/Technology.

The Group traces its roots to1995, initially starting as a cocoa and palm
oil trading business. Since then, we have been participating in strategic
opportunities to generate growth over time and bring scalable, positive
impact towards the communities and environment.
